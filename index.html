What is ZoneOut about?
ZoneOut is a YSWS where you create something themed around horror, mystery or ARG!
What is the theme?
Horror, mystery and ARG focusing on immersiveness and storytelling. [and, of course, good art!]
What kind of projects are to be submitted?
Projects can be:

• A website
• A terminal / CLI program
• A game
• A mod (eg. Minecraft)
Basically, anything that can be spooky.
This event is entirely software!
How do I track time?
Use Hackatime. HackClub's own time-tracking tool!
What does the shop have?
Custom merch and stickers along with high hacker value items! Recommendations would be appreciated in the #1zone-out channel!
